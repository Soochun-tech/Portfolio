## 🗂️ Projects

### 🧠 [Uncertainty Estimation in Neural Networks — Deep Ensemble](./deep-ensemble-project)
> **Python · PyTorch** | Deep Ensemble · EDL · Bayesian DL | Uncertainty Quantification · OOD Detection

As part of an ongoing **UROP research project** at the University of Minnesota, implemented and compared three uncertainty quantification strategies (Baseline CNN, EDL, Deep Ensemble) on MNIST as a prototype for PLAsTiCC astronomical light-curve classification.

- Reduced generalization gap from **4.5% → 2%** through Deep Ensemble implementation
- Observed **20x increase in Mutual Information** and **10x increase in Predictive Entropy** under perturbation, confirming reliable uncertainty estimation
- Conducted input perturbation experiments (Gaussian noise, rotation) to validate uncertainty response
- Deep Ensemble achieved the best generalization and most reliable uncertainty response among all three methods

### 🔍 [Identifying High-Risk Ethereum Accounts Through Multimethod Outlier Detection](./ethereum-project)
> **R** | Isolation Forest · Z-score · IQR · LASSO | Fraud Detection · AML

Applied and compared four outlier detection techniques to identify potentially fraudulent accounts across 4,000+ Ethereum accounts with 50 features.

- Demonstrated that a combined **Isolation Forest + LASSO pipeline** offers the best balance between detection coverage and operational efficiency
- Isolation Forest anomaly scores showed strongest correlation with `total_transactions` (r = 0.876)
- Directly applicable to real-world **AML compliance systems** and crypto fraud monitoring

### 🎬 [Recommending Movies using Duration, Rating, and Year](./movie-recommendation-clustering)
> **R** | K-means · Hierarchical Clustering · GMM | Unsupervised Learning · Recommender Systems

Clustered 8,800 IMDB movies using unsupervised learning to identify patterns in audience ratings.

- Identified that movies with **120+ minute duration** and release year after 2012 consistently achieved the highest average ratings (6.82–6.90) across all three clustering methods
- Proposed a two-step recommendation strategy — K-means/Hierarchical for initial segmentation, GMM for refined personalization
- Directly applicable to cold-start recommendation problems on streaming platforms like Netflix
